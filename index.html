PORTFOLIO

I dedicated myself to tattooing, and many other things form of art. Even though for some people this is only a hobby, I find that creating art is good for me. Tattoing helped me better understand myself during my anxious and depressive periods. I often reflect on what went wrong, what I did wrong, and whether or not my actions were morally and ethically correct. It seems as though the tattoo machine gave me access to my inner self. The more strokes I make, the less anxious I feel.

I became a fan of art when I was six years old. When I entered college, I intended to major in fine arts, but the cost was prohibitive for me. Despite my lack of resources, I continued to pursue it as my favorite interest.

Through art, I was able to connect with people in a community and be myself. Great artists are all around me, and I am humbled to see them on their shows. I don't feel insignificant, though; on the contrary, it motivated me to work harder and aim for excellence the next time I pick up a tattoo machine, Below is my sample works.

Painting

Painting, the expression of ideas and emotions, with the creation of certain aesthetic qualities, in a two-dimensional visual language. The elements of this language—its shapes, lines, colours, tones, and textures—are used in various ways to produce sensations of volume, space, movement, and light on a flat surface

Pentration Testing

A penetration test (pen test) is an authorized simulated attack performed on a computer system to evaluate its security. Penetration testers use the same tools, techniques, and processes as attackers to find and demonstrate the business impacts of weaknesses in a system. Penetration tests usually simulate a variety of attacks that could threaten a business.
